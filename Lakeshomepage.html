Blue Kayak Adventures
This page contains resource links and general information for my attempt to fish every lake and river mile in Waukesha County. For specific lake data and personal fishing logs, follow the "Lake Data" link.

Links

  • WI DNR Lakes Page

    The WI DNR page contains basic contour maps, fish species notes, public access notes, a GIS viewer, boating and fishing regulations links, and lake survey details. It is a great resource to access before trying out a new lake.

  • LakeLink

    Wisconsin Fishing reports on Lake-Link.

  • Waukesha County GIS Parcel Map

    This parcel map is a useful resource to find access points to bodies of water that may not have public landings. If you have a portable boat, you can launch from nearly any public land.

  • Lake Data Spreadsheet

    This spreadsheet contains personal, detailed fishing reports.
