Blue Kayak Adventures
This page give information on streams I've fished, Wisconsin trout resources, and my broader attempts to catch a trophy of every species of trout, salmon, char, and grayling.

Links

  • WI DNR Trout Maps page

    This is one of the best resources when scouting for new water. Every body of water classified as holding trout in Wisconsin will be listed on one of these maps.

  • Wisconsin Trout Streams (Blue book)

    Document listing all trout stream miles in WI. including species present, miles classified, and wild reproduction or stocked.

  • WI DNR Fish Stocking database

    Use this link to review fish stocking for any body of water across the state in the last several decades. It normally is updates every several months, so the information may not include the most recent stockings.

  • Published Fisheries Surveys

    This page lists fisheries surveys from the last several years. Older surveys may not be included, as well as unpublished fisheries survey data. However, it remains a useful resource, especially for better known fisheries.
